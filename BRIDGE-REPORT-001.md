### Lookout — Synthesis
**Conclusion**: The optimal ESP32 chess heuristic is:
1. **Primary**: Capture the highest-value piece available
2. **Secondary**: Develop knights and bishops early
3. **Tertiary**: Control center squares

This maps directly to Helmsman's PTX kernel — the board array uses piece values (pawn=100, knight=320, bishop=330, rook=500, queen=900, king=20000). Material evaluation is already the fastest and most effective heuristic at low search depth.
